About Activity Recording System

Inspired by the opportunities ahead and frustrated by the behaviours that hold us back. This application will allow you to report and bear witness to any activity you are aware of taking place in the world. You may have strong views on the merits or undesirability of these activities and you should explain why you have that opinion. Just please respect others opinions on the site as well. We are looking to collate and highlight activities that apppear to either be hindering or aiding progress. Hopefully highlight he best and the worst in each area will both encouraging and supporting the good to increase their impact and help ensure the bad are aware of the consensus and to encourage them to change their behaviour.

Approach

The approach is based on an open crowdsourced platform where people can report activity they like or dislkie and others can review, discuss and rate the activity. Users can select their scope and preferences by geography and category. We have loosely folllowed the top 10 of the UN development goals in setting the categories.

At present the system supports the following items:

  1. Activity Reporting
  2. Rating of reported activities
  3. Discussion

While the system is primarily passive at present with a watching brief to complement networked decision making it may not always stay that way. If we achieve progress in life extending healthcare but lack the resources to deliver this to all AND we have obtained global consensus that behaviour should be a factor in healthcare entitlement then conceivably a future version of this system may bear witness to that behaviour and be used to determine entitlement to life extending treatment.

Privacy

Personal privacy is important. This system clearly has potential and definite intention to put a spotlight on personal activities which could conflict with this. We will provide facilities to allow people to challenge or corroborate activities and will track the extent of corroboration or dispute attached to any report We will also not disclose the source of any reported activity without authorisation.
